OrganisationFunktion
  - Attribute fields that MUST be filled out
    ... none ...
  - Relations that must NOT be filled out
    - TilknyttedeInteresseFaellesskaber
    - TilknyttedePersoner
  - Relations that MUST be filled out
    - Funktionstype (and it must be validated that this points to one of the four known types)
      - the four known types are POSITION, IT_USAGE, PAYOUT_UNIT, CONTACT_UNIT
After this, we validate depending on the type
  - Type = POSITION
    - Allowed Relationships
       - TilknyttedeBrugere (optional, so 0 or more is allowed)
       - TilknytedeEnheder (exactly 1 requried)       
    - Prohibited Relationships
       - TilknyttedeItSystemer
       - Opgaver
  - Type = IT_USAGE
    - Allowed Relationships
       - TilknyttedeItSystemer (exactly 1 required)
	   - TilknytedeEnheder
    - Prohibited Relationships
       - Opgaver
       - TilknyttedeBrugere
  - Type = PAYOUT_UNIT
    - Allowed Relationships
       - TilknytedeEnheder (exactly 1 is required)
         - the OrgUnit that is referenced must be read, and it must be validated that the Unit has a LOSShortName address relationship
    - Prohibited Relationships
       - TilknyttedeItSystemer
       - Opgaver
       - TilknyttedeBrugere
  - TYPE = CONTACT_UNIT
    - Allowed Relationships
       - Opgaver (at least 1 relationship is required, more is allowed)
       - TilknytedeEnheder (exactly 1 is required)
    - Prohibited Relationships
       - TilknyttedeItSystemer
       - TilknyttedeBrugere
